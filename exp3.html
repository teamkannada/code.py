KUVEMPU

Kuvempu, whose full name was Kuppalli Venkatappa Puttappa (1904-1994),
was a highly influential Kannada poet, novelist, playwright, and critic, widely regarded as the greatest Kannada poet of the 20th century.
He is also known as "Rashtrakavi" (National Poet) and "Karnataka Ratna" (Gem of Karnataka).
